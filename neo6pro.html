Advanced Driver Technology

The Neo 6 Pro features an advanced triple dual balanced armature (BA) speaker driver configuration in each ear. This arrangement enables exceptional audio performance across the entire frequency spectrum.

With low impedance and high sensitivity, it delivers powerful, full-range sound even when driven by low-output audio devices.

Engineered for maximum clarity, headroom, and balance, providing distortion-free performance even at high SPLs.
